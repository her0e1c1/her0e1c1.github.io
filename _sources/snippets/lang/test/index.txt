index.scm
===========================

To test function sample, type `'M-x ert RET`'

.. code-block:: sh
   

    $ ee '(progn (require '\''ert) (ert-deftest sample () (should (eq 1 1))))' 
    sample


.. code-block:: sh
   

    $ ee '(progn (require '\''ert) (ert-deftest test-divide-by-zero () (should-error (/ 1 0) :type '\''arith-error)))' 
    test-divide-by-zero
