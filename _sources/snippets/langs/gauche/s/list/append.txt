

append!
=======

末尾に要素を加える

.. code-block:: sh
   

    $ gosh -e '(print (begin (define a '\''(1 2)) (append! a '\''(3))))' -Eexit
    (1 2 3)
    


.. warning::

    NULLの場合は書き変わらないので注意


.. code-block:: sh
   

    $ gosh -e '(print (begin (define a '\''()) (append! a '\''(1))))' -Eexit
    (1)
