

map
===


.. code-block:: sh
   

    $ gosh -e '(print (begin (map cons '\''(1 2 3) '\''(4 5 6))))' -Eexit
    ((1 . 4) (2 . 5) (3 . 6))
    

１つ以上のリストを引数に取れる

.. code-block:: sh
   

    $ gosh -e '(print (begin (map (lambda (x y) (+ x y)) '\''(1 2 3) '\''(4 5 6))))' -Eexit
    (5 7 9)
    


.. code-block:: sh
   

    $ gosh -e "(print (begin (map + (iota 5) (iota 5))))" -Eexit
    (0 2 4 6 8)
